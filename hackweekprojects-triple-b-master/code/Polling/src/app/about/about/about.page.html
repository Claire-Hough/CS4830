About Home Page

The home page displays all of the open polls which users can vote on.

Users can click on a poll they want to view.

Users are allowed to vote once per poll.

Users will have the option to end polls that they create, which will remove them from the Home page and display the completed results on the Results page.

 Create a New Poll

Each user who is logged-in is allowed to submit polls for others to vote on.

Users need to provide the embedded URL from the YouTube video of their choice, the question they want users to vote on, and two options that will appear on
the voting buttons.

 Poll Results Page

Results from closed polls are displayed on this page.

Poll results are displayed here when users close the polls they've created.

 Login Page

Users can login using their Google account, or another email.

Only logged in users can create and vote on polls.

 About Us

People often form strong opinions that are reinforced when they associate with like-minded individuals. We wanted to shine light on differing opinions by allowing people to see the
true majority opinion on certain topics as well as give people the opportunity to see what they have in common.
